Recipe 4: Crab Rangoon

Crab Rangoon

Ingredients

  • 30 Wonton Wrappers
  • Â½ cup of Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 300g of Fresh Crab Meat
  • 3 stalks of Green Onion (chopped)
  • 250g of Cream Cheese
  • 6 tbsp of Japanese Mayo
  • 1 tbsp of Sesame Oil
  • 1 tbsp of Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp of Salt
  • 2.5 tbsp of Soy Sauce

    • Directions

  • Mix Crab Meat, Cream Cheese, Japanese Mayo, Black Pepper, Salt, Soy Sauce, and Green onion in a bowl. Once everything is combined, transfer to a baking pan.
  • Cover with shredded mozzarella cheese and bake at 375 for 20 minutes
  • Cut the wonton wrapper diagonally, and in a pan, heat oil. Once the oil is hot, add in the wonton wrapper and fry for 30 seconds on each side or until golden brown and crispy. Make sure not to over-fry the chips, as after you remove them from the pan, the chip will still darken. Rest the wonton chips on a paper towel and season with salt
  • Serve
